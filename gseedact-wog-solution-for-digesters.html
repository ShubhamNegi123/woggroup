WOG Technologies - Gseedact+ Solutions

WOG Granulated Seeding sludge is another product which plays a vital role in Water and treatment process by enhancing Digestors startup &ensure its best performance.

Although Start-up of anaerobic reactor is affected by several parameters such as reactor configuration, wastewater characteristics, environmental conditionsbut microbial development plays a vital role in Digester Performance.

W.O.G provided Seeding sludge is best suited for Digesters for various Industrial Effluent due to its high microbial activity and congeniality which convert the organic content of the wastewater into biogas.

It can be used as a triggering of anaerobes in high concentration organic wastewater treatment system. WOG granule sludge is a form of microbial immobilization, appearance as regular round or oval and color are black or dark gray, with high intensity and good slurry separation and good settling property

WOG Seeding sludge are characterized for their biomass characteristics, for example, volatile suspended solids (VSS) and total suspended solids (TSS), specific glucose utilization (SGU), specific methanogenic activity (SMA), and microbial community
