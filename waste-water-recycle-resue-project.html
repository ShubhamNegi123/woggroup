WOG Technologies - Waste Water Recycle/Resue Project - Phuket, Thailand

W.O.G Group has executed a Waste Water Treatment Recycle/Reuse Project capacity 25MLD in Thailand to increase municipal water supply in Phuket with an objective to meet the Thai Tap water quality standards set by Provincial Water authority (PWA).

Treatment Scheme: Flash Mixer – Flocculator – Lamella Clarifier – Gravity Sand Filters – Ultra Filtration System – Micron Cartridge Filters – Two Stage of Brackish RO (90 % Recovery) to produce Tap Water Quality

Scope of Work: The scope of work included capital investment, complete design, engineering, manufacturing, supply, installation, commissioning, start-up, and performance and reliability tests, operation and Maintenance of the plant.

Impact on Society and Environment

  • This mega project will provide clear water to the municipality at an agreed rate, one lower than that charged by PWA, under the terms of a 30-years Build-Operate- Transfer agreement
