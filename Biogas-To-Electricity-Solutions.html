WOG Technologies - Biogas To Electricity Solutions

For the last few years , we have focused on the Biogas based captive power generation projects in decentralised manner. The gas potential is generally available in high organic waste/wastewater water from municipal and industrial both. The produced gas is used to generate the electricity running the DG sets on dual fuel mode by the Operator. Electricity produced can be posted to grid for Electricity utilization if in excess from the industrial usages.
