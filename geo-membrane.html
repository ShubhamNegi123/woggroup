WOG Technologies - 3 Layers Geo Membrane

WOG-SR 3 layers Geo-membrane products are having unique features like Impressive dimensional stability, tensile strength and puncture resistance.

WOG-SR 3 layers Geo-membrane are of :

  • An Ethylene Inter-polymer Alloy (EIA) which has high strength and chemically resistant for maximum resistance to high temperature, and broad chemical resistance, including acids, oils, methane & H.S. Stability is excellent, with low thermal expansion-contraction properties.
  • Ethafoam at the centre, a strong, resilient, medium-density, closed-cell polyethylene foam.
  • High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) is an excellent product for large applications that require UV and Ozone resistance, chemical resistance or high-quality installations

WOG-SR 3 layers Geo-membrane is used in all types of applications including Pond Liners, Secondary Containment, Floating Covers, Gas Collection & Storages, Critical Waste Water Baffles and Potable Water Containment.

The key to the system is the gas impermeable layer of WOG-SR geo-membrane which serves as the floating or tightly fixed cover. The outside of the cover is constantly exposed to environmental conditions such as sun, wind, UV rays, precipitation, and temperature variations. The underside of the WOG-SR cover is exposed to high concentrations of oils and grease, high liquid temperatures, varying ambient temperatures and constantly changing liquid levels.

Applications of WOG-SR 3 layers Geo-membrane:

  • Floating covers-Gas collections, Odor controls.
  • Leachate ponds.
  • Secondary containment.
  • Process wastewater.
  • Floating baffles.
  • Tank floors.
  • Oil booms.
  • Wastewater ponds
